About VidDown

VidDown is an open-source desktop downloader for creators, researchers, and anyone who needs a reliable local tool for saving videos and audio.

Built with Electron, React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, VidDown gives you a modern UI, concurrent downloads, and the ability to cancel or retry downloads instantly.

Use it responsibly and only download content you are permitted to save.
